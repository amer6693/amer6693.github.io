BNM Regulatory Sandbox

Bank Negara Malaysia seeks to provide a regulatory environment that is conducive for the deployment of fintech solutions. This includes reviewing and adapting regulatory requirements or procedures that may unintentionally inhibit innovation. Hence, the Sandbox was introduced in 2016 to enable innovation of fintech to be deployed and tested in a live environment, with specified parameters and timeframes.

Resources for Potential Sandbox Applicants

Sandbox Application Guidance
The Fintech Regulatory Sandbox guidelines sets out the objective and principles of the Sandbox, and provide guidance to potential applicants on the application process, the main evaluation criteria and the information to be furnished to BNM.
Regulated Entities
Participating fintech companies intending to carry out regulated businesses will be assessed based on applicable licensing, approval and registration criteria under the FSA, IFSA and MSBA, as the case may be.
Fintech Booster
Spearheaded by Bank Negara Malaysia (the Bank) and Malaysia Digital Economy Corporation (MDEC), the quarterly Fintech Booster programme is a toolkit of capacitybuilding resources for companies to develop their fintech solutions to a proof of concept (POC) stage

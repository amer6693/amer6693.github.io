Sandbox Guidance to Potential Applicants

The guidance provided in this section seeks to facilitate better understanding of the general criteria assessed by the Bank to determine the eligibility of potential applicants to participate in BNMâ€™s Regulatory Sandbox.Interested applicants should take note of this guidance accordingly.

Sandbox Application Stages

Before applying to the Sandbox

  • Finalise the business model
  • Go through the Fintech Regulatory Sandbox Framework
  • Determine if the business activity is regulated by BNM
Stage 1: Application

  • Applicant will be assessed thoroughly against the eligibility criteria and proposed safeguards for testing
  • Applicant will be informed of its eligibility within 15 working days from the submission of complete application
Stage 2: Preparation

  • Participant will work with the Bank on the details of the testing parameters, measures to determine success or failure, exit strategy and transition plan before the commencement of the test.
Stage 3: Testing

  • Participant may start testing the product, service or solution upon obtaining the Bank's approval
  • Participant may be required to submit information
  • Participant must submit interim reports on the progress of the test and final report at the end of the test

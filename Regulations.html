Regulations

To enable the Bank to meet the objectives of a central bank, it is vested with comprehensive legal powers under the following legislation to regulate and supervise the financial system.

Key Regulatory Objective

Safety and soundness of financial services provider is key to maintaining consumer trust and confidence in the financial system

Market Conduct

Objective

  • Promoting fair and equitable market practices
  • Promoting the financial capabilities of consumers
Prudential

Objective

  • Able to withstand adverse economic condition and maintain customer confidence
  • Address moral hazards by mitigating excessive risk-taking behaviour
  • Protection of depositors and policyholders by preventing mismanagement of funds

Key components of prudential regulation

  • Ensure sufficient liquidity and capital adequacy
  • Good corporate governance
  • Sound risk management and limits
